<h1> YellowBord-Run </h1>

Inspired by the classic arcade spirit of Out Run, this game delivers a fast-paced driving experience where precision and reflexes are key. Players take control of a high-speed car racing along endless roads, shifting left and right to dodge incoming traffic and obstacles.

As the journey intensifies, the roads become more crowded and unpredictable, testing the player’s ability to react under pressure. Every collision has consequences—your vehicle visibly deteriorates with each impact, adding a layer of tension and realism to the gameplay.

With its retro-inspired design and straightforward yet addictive mechanics, the game captures the thrill of classic arcade racing while introducing dynamic visual damage that reflects your performance on the road. How long can you survive before your car gives in?
